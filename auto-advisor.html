Cost model assumptions

Depreciation: archetype-specific profile. New cars lose ~18–22 % year 1, declining. EVs (non-Tesla) and German luxury cars depreciate fastest. Body-on-frame trucks and Toyota hybrids depreciate slowest.

Fuel / energy: annual km × consumption × current price. EV consumption is in kWh/100 km including ~10 % charging losses.

Insurance: RCA (mandatory liability, RO bands) + Casco (full hull, ~3.5 % of car value/yr, scaled by archetype risk).

Road tax (impozit auto): RO formula by engine displacement bands. EVs: €0 (still exempt). Hybrids: −95 % discount.

Maintenance: archetype-specific yearly estimate, scaled up for older cars and luxury brands.

Tires: one set every ~50 000 km amortized; second set if winter tires required by use case.

ITP: ~€30 every 2 years (newer cars), every year for older.

NOT included: finance interest, RAR registration one-off, parking, fines, accidents, RCA-CASS surcharges, opportunity cost of capital. Verify all RO tax figures with ANAF or your insurer before buying.
