How the Romanian tax model works

Annual drag (each year):

  • TER deducted from gross return (e.g., 8% − 0.07% = 7.93% net of fund fee)
  • Dividend yield × (1 − div tax) reinvested; the dividend tax portion is paid out

At exit (final year):

  • Capital gains tax applied to (final value − total cost basis), where cost basis = starting amount + sum of contributions
  • If gain is negative, no CGT

CASS: Romanian health contribution, capped flat fee based on annual investment income vs. minimum-wage thresholds. Modeled as €490 (low band) up to €1,960 (cap) per year, applied across the holding period and paid out of returns.

What's NOT modeled: IBKR commissions (small), FX spread (RON ↔ EUR), 15% US dividend WHT (already absorbed at fund level before VUSA pays you), partial-year sells, loss carry-forward, the 1%/3% preferential rate (only applies via Romanian brokers like XTB Romania).

Always verify with ANAF or a fiscal consultant before filing.
